# Privacy Policy — WIR Lineup Builder

**Last updated: March 27, 2026**

## Overview
WIR Lineup Builder ("the App") is a companion tool for War Inc Rising players. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Username (callsign) — chosen by you
- Password — stored securely using bcrypt hashing (we cannot see your actual password)

### Usage Data
- Troop roster data you enter (troop names, levels)
- Lineups you create and save
- Number of optimizer generations used

### Advertising Data
The free version of the app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data

For more information about Google's data practices, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide the lineup building and optimization features
- To save your roster and lineups to your account
- To display relevant advertisements (free version only)
- To process in-app purchases

## Data Storage
Your data is stored on secure servers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## In-App Purchases
The app offers a one-time PRO upgrade for $5.99 processed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information directly.

## Your Rights
You may:
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Export your data using the in-app export feature
- Contact us with questions about your data

## Third-Party Services
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Google Play / Apple App Store (in-app purchases)
- Railway (server hosting)

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact: warincbuilder@gmail.com

## Disclaimer
WIR Lineup Builder is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to War Inc Rising or its developers. All game data is community-sourced.
