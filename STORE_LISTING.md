## Full Description
The ultimate lineup building and optimization tool for War Inc Rising players.

**BUILD SMARTER LINEUPS**
Place your troops on a full 7×7 battlefield grid. Choose from Arena, Hunting, Adventure, Infinite War, Clan War, and Clan Hunt modes. Select formations (Dash, Backstab, Outflank, Split) and see their strengths and weaknesses.

**70 TROOPS — COMPLETE DATABASE**
Every troop in the game with full stats: HP, ATK at all 9 levels, Defense, Movement Speed, Attack Speed, Class, Attribute, Weakness, Abilities, and upgrade descriptions. Tap any troop for the complete breakdown.

**SMART OPTIMIZER**
Tell the optimizer what mode you're playing, what boss you're fighting, or what formation you're facing — it recommends the best lineup from YOUR roster. It factors in troop levels, ability unlocks, attribute counters, formation synergies, and role balance.

**HUNTING BOSS COUNTER-PICKS**
Fighting Twin-Dragon or Evil Ivy? The optimizer knows their weaknesses and resistances and picks troops that exploit them.

**TRACK YOUR ROSTER**
Add your troops with their levels. Add duplicates for multiple copies. See your total roster power and how each troop grows with levels.

**SAVE & COMPARE LINEUPS**
Save your best lineups for each game mode. Compare power ratings and team compositions.

**Features:**
• 70 troops with complete stats at all 9 levels
• 7×7 battlefield grid with all game modes
• Arena formation picker with counter-matchup analysis
• Hunting boss counter-pick system
• Ability-aware optimization (knows which abilities are unlocked at your level)
• Adjustable elixir limits (50-1000)
• Multiple roster copies of the same troop
• Export/import data for backup
